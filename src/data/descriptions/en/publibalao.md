Publibalão has been operating since 1994 — Portugal's first hot air balloon company, and still among the most active. Based in Fronteira, in the Alentejo, they run free flights, tethered flights, night glow displays, team building experiences, pilot training, and wedding services. They've completed over 4,000 flights in Portugal and abroad.

They also organise FIBAQ — the Festival Internacional de Balões de Ar Quente — which since its first edition in 1997 has grown into one of Europe's largest ballooning festivals, drawing teams from across the continent each November.

Plura built a site that handles the full scope of what Publibalão does: the commercial offer, the festival with its own dedicated section, a voucher system, a photo gallery, and location mapping for flight zones. A lot of moving parts for a company that spends most of its time in the air.
