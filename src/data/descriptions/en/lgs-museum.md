A companion site to the main Sociedade de Geografia de Lisboa website, the Museum site focuses specifically on the Society's collections and exhibitions. It presents objects from around the world — ethnographic pieces, cartographic materials, portraits, and artefacts from Portugal's age of exploration — through a structured catalogue.

The site also covers visiting information, loans, research, and patronage, functioning as both a public-facing showcase and a resource for researchers and institutions interested in accessing the collection.

Plura developed the museum site in close relation to the main institutional site, ensuring a shared visual language across both digital presences.
