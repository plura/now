Vetocular is a veterinary ophthalmology service founded and led by Dr. Ana Isabel Monteiro. Rather than operating from a fixed clinic, Vetocular works as a mobile service — travelling to veterinary practices, clinics, and hospitals across the Lisbon area to provide specialist eye consultations, diagnostic exams, and surgical procedures where the animal is already being seen.

The offer is comprehensive: full ophthalmological examinations, electroretinography, ocular ultrasound, gonioscopy, and a range of surgical procedures from eyelid correction to cataract surgery by phacoemulsification. The site documents the service clearly for referring vets, and includes an active social feed of clinical cases and educational content.

Plura developed the site alongside Empire Creative, building a clean professional platform for a specialist service with a very clear sense of what it does and for whom.
