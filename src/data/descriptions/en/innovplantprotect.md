InnovPlantProtect (InPP) is a collaborative laboratory based in Elvas, working at the intersection of agricultural science and innovation. The organisation develops biosolutions for plant health, digital tools for precision agriculture, and customised laboratory services — with a network of partners that includes the University of Évora, Syngenta, Bayer, and a range of Portuguese research and agricultural institutions.

The site communicates the organisation's dual identity: rigorous scientific institution and active industry partner. It includes news, events, recruitment, and a clear breakdown of services, reflecting an organisation that is growing fast and wants to be found by the right people.

Plura developed a site that gives a research-led organisation a confident, outward-facing presence.
