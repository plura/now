The Centro de Informação de Biotecnologia (CiB) was founded in 2002 with a mission to promote and communicate biotechnology in Portugal, across Portuguese-speaking countries, and in Europe. It publishes a steady stream of articles, interviews, and opinion pieces on topics ranging from CRISPR and genetic editing to GMOs and agricultural innovation — making it a go-to reference for anyone following the field in the Portuguese language.

The site aggregates the organisation's full output: blog, press, video, events, publications, useful links, and legislation. It's built more like an editorial platform than a traditional institutional site, which fits an organisation whose primary output is information.

Plura developed a site that keeps pace with an organisation that publishes several times a week and needs a clean, navigable home for it all.
