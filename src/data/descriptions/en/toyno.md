Toyno is a Lisbon-based space and experience design studio. Their work focuses on creating environments for public, work, education, and leisure spaces — grounded in the belief that spaces are living systems that shape how people share, collaborate, and participate. They also run workshops and have developed a research project called *Spaces Between Us*.

The site is as much a manifesto as a portfolio — minimal and typographic, leading with the studio's ethos before showing the projects. It's bilingual in Portuguese and English.

Plura developed the web presence for a studio that thinks seriously about how spaces work, and wanted a site that reflected that thinking.
