Diogo Stilwell is one of Portugal's most awarded copywriters, with a career spanning Ogilvy, Wunderman Thompson, and Dentsu Creative. His portfolio includes campaigns for Betclic, Vodafone, MEO, Visa, CNN, The History Channel, and the City of Lisbon — work that ranges from sport and culture to social causes and international brands.

The site is a personal portfolio with a distinct voice and character. A running counter on the homepage — "Nº1 Copywriter in Portugal. For now. Ranking reset:" — sets the tone immediately: confident, self-aware, a little playful. The work is presented cleanly, letting the campaigns speak.
