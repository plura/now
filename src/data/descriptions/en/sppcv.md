The Sociedade Portuguesa de Patologia da Coluna Vertebral (SPPCV) was founded in 2003 to bring together clinicians from different specialties working in the field of spine pathology. It runs an annual national congress, publishes a journal, promotes Iberian training partnerships with its Spanish counterpart, and runs an active programme of opinion pieces, press releases, and public health campaigns.

The website is the society's main communication platform — covering events, news, membership, publications, and eLearning. It serves both the medical community and the general public, balancing clinical rigour with accessible outreach on topics like spine health, obesity, and preventive movement.

Plura built a site that reflects the breadth of what a medical society actually does — far more than just running a congress once a year.
