Galeria Reverso is a Lisbon-based gallery dedicated to contemporary art jewellery — a field that sits between fine art and craft, and has its own distinct international community. The gallery shows established international names alongside emerging talents, with exhibitions, publications, and an online shop.

The site functions as both gallery and archive: past and current exhibitions are documented in detail, artists have dedicated profile pages, and the shop makes pieces available internationally. An ongoing series called *Trajectories* traces the development of individual artistic practices over time.

Plura built a site that gives a niche but serious gallery a digital presence worthy of the work it shows.
