Eduardo Paz Ferreira & Associados is a Lisbon law firm founded by Professor Eduardo Paz Ferreira, a full professor at the Faculty of Law of the University of Lisbon and one of Portugal's most respected voices in tax law, public finance, and European economic law. The firm covers a wide range of practice areas, from arbitration and corporate governance to banking law, regulatory affairs, and financial markets.

The site serves as both a professional platform and a public-facing record of the professor's extensive career — including publications, events, lectures, and media appearances. A news section keeps it current, documenting the firm's activity and Eduardo Paz Ferreira's ongoing civic and academic work.

The design reflects the firm's gravitas without being stiff — it's a site that speaks to institutional clients while remaining accessible to the wider public.
