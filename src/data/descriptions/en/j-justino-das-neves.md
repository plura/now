J. Justino das Neves is a family company with over 70 years of history, based in Fátima and specialising in the wholesale distribution of steel products and a retail DIY store. It's the kind of business where reputation is built across generations — and the site was built to honour that.

Designed by Catarina Zimbarra and developed by Plura, the website tells the company's story honestly: where it came from, what it sells, and who it serves. It covers the full product catalogue, the company's history, team, and values, while keeping the focus on the practical — stock, contacts, and how to request a quote.

It's a corporate site that doesn't try to be more than it needs to be, and is all the better for it.
