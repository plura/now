Basecamp is a production services company supplying equipment, crew, and logistics to the film and advertising industry in Portugal. Their offer covers an unusually broad range — from craft services and grip to generators, radios, lighting, location protection, and even a fully equipped Kit Van — serving everything from large-scale film productions to smaller commercial shoots.

The site is built with the industry in mind: clean, professional, and easy to navigate for producers and production managers who need to know quickly what's available. It includes a full product catalogue organised by category, services overview, and portfolio of past productions.

Plura developed a site that keeps up with a company that works at the pace of a film set.
