JoMI is the annual Internal Medicine conference organised by the Internal Medicine Department of Hospital de Santa Maria, in Lisbon. The site was built for the 2025 edition, which brought together clinicians over three days at the Centro Ismaili, with a programme covering obesity, cardiovascular risk, artificial intelligence in healthcare, palliative care, and personalised medicine — among other topics.

Beyond the programme, the site handled the full logistics of the event: registration with tiered pricing across different professional categories, pre-conference course bookings, and abstract submission with its own structured form and guidelines.

It's a focused, functional event site that does exactly what it needs to do — get the right people to the right place with the right information.
