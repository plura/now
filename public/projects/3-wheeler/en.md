3E-Cargo is a brand of light electric vehicles built for urban logistics — cargo bikes and electric three-wheelers designed for last-mile delivery and passenger transport in city environments. The range covers three vehicle types: a Pick Up for cargo, a Trunk for larger loads, and a Cab for urban passenger transport. All vehicles come with European type approval.

The site is a showcase and e-commerce platform in one, available in six languages (English, Portuguese, French, Spanish, Dutch, and German), reflecting the brand's European ambitions. It covers the vehicle range, specifications, gallery, and includes a booking system for test rides.

Plura built a site that gives a sustainable mobility brand the kind of clean, confident presence the sector demands.
