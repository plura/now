CETERA is an academic clinical research organisation (CRO) linked to the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Lisbon, through AIDFM. It manages clinical trials and research projects across a range of therapeutic areas — handling everything from project management and regulatory support to patient recruitment, monitoring, and clinical advisory services.

The site is bilingual (English and Portuguese) and pitched squarely at international pharma and research partners. It communicates credibility through numbers — researchers, sites, participants, recruitment success rates — and through testimonials from partners at institutions including the Montreal Heart Institute and Lisbon's Santa Maria Hospital.

Plura developed the site in collaboration with Studio Catarina Zimbarra, building a professional platform that positions CETERA confidently on the international clinical research stage.
