Nova Sotecma has been a fixture of Angolan industry for over 30 years, supplying equipment, tools, and technical solutions across construction, maintenance, welding, energy, and industrial protection. More than a distributor, the company is the official service centre in Angola for several global manufacturers, and counts brands like Husqvarna, Grundfos, SKF, Nilfisk, Fluke, and ESAB among its partners.

The website reflects the scale and confidence of a business that has earned its place in the market through decades of work on the ground. It includes an online shop, product catalogues, news, and technical support information — everything a professional buyer needs in one place.

The site is bilingual in Portuguese and English, and serves both local clients and international partners looking to understand the scope of what Nova Sotecma brings to the Angolan market.
