CCUL — Centro Cardiovascular da Universidade de Lisboa — is the cardiovascular research centre of the Faculty of Medicine of the University of Lisbon. It brings together multiple research units and platforms, runs advanced training programmes, and invests in cardiovascular literacy through school programmes, a public app, and community events.

The site is in English, reflecting the centre's international research profile. It covers research groups, platforms, publications, training, and knowledge transfer initiatives, with a structure designed for both academic peers and a broader public interested in cardiovascular health.

Plura developed the site alongside designer Catarina Zimbarra, creating a rigorous but approachable platform for one of Portugal's leading biomedical research centres.
