The Centro Paroquial de São Bernardo, based in Aveiro, has been part of the local community for over 50 years. It runs a nursery, pre-school, after-school centre, residential care for the elderly, home support services, and a day centre — serving around 500 families a year, from children of four months to residents in their hundreds.

The website reflects the warmth and depth of what the centre actually does. It covers all the service areas, registration information, weekly menus, volunteer opportunities, and a donation platform. Testimonials from families — some of whom have had children, parents, and grandparents pass through the centre across generations — give the site a genuinely human quality.

Plura built a site that feels like the organisation it represents: grounded, caring, and deeply embedded in its community.
