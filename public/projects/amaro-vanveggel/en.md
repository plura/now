Amaro & Van Veggel is a multinational consultancy group operating across real estate, construction, capital management, and advisory services. The firm was co-founded by Daniel Amaro, an entrepreneur with deep roots in the Portuguese construction industry, and Bernardo van Veggel, a finance professional with experience spanning Europe and the United States. Their pitch is simple: one trusted partner for the full scope of real estate decisions.

The site presents the group's four divisions — Real Estate, Construction, Capital Management, and Consultancy — in a clear, professional structure. Available in five languages (English, Portuguese, French, Spanish, and Russian), it's built to serve an international clientele, with each section communicating trust and long-term partnership.
