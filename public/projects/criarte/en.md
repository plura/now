Cri.ar.te is a creative studio in Lisbon run by a clinical psychologist and a creative educator, offering workshops for children built around art, storytelling, movement, and sensory experience. The premise is that creative expression is one of the most effective tools children have for developing emotional resilience, self-confidence, and the capacity to handle frustration.

The site introduces the studio and its founders, explains the different workshop formats — from creative art and nature-focused sessions to hidden story workshops and emotion-based activities — and opens the door to private events and birthday parties. It's a warm, inviting site that feels like the people behind it.

Plura developed the site for a small, purpose-led studio doing meaningful work with young children.
