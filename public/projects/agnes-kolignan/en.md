Agnes Kolignan is a freelance designer and artist working across print, embroidery, and jewellery. Her work lives somewhere between fashion and fine art, with a strong material sensibility and an interest in heritage, texture, and cultural reference. She also shows her work in exhibitions and sells through an Etsy shop.

The portfolio site presents her practice through collections, materials, collaborations, and topics — a structure that reflects the layered nature of her work rather than a simple project list. An exhibitions section documents her shows over time.

Plura developed a portfolio site that gives an artist with a distinctive voice a digital home that doesn't flatten it.
