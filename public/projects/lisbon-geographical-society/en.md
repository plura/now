The Sociedade de Geografia de Lisboa was founded in 1875, making it one of the oldest scientific and cultural societies in Portugal. Based in its historic building on Rua das Portas de Santo Antão in Lisbon, it brings together researchers, professionals, and curious minds across more than 20 specialist sections — from geography and history to anthropology, ethnography, and arts.

The website serves the society's full scope of activity: history, upcoming events, membership, library and cartography archive access, shop, and the separate museum site. It's a bilingual site (Portuguese and English) that balances institutional weight with genuine accessibility — making a 150-year-old institution feel present and approachable.

Plura built both this site and the accompanying museum site, creating a coherent digital home for one of Lisbon's most storied cultural institutions.
